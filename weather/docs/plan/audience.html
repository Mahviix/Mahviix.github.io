Targeted audience

My targeted audience for this website are indiduals who are curious about current weathers and weathers they should look forward to. The weather is important to have a knownlege of because this determines most of the actiities of the day, what types of cloths to wear, etc.People who actualy come online to check the internet for information about the weather are young and middle age adults, some teenages who are usually bothered about the weather conditions also do so. So my main targeted audience then ranges from ages 35 to 70.

Personas

Name: Marvel jack

Interested in the fashion, cars and sporting activitie. A morning person and usually looks
at the weather condition before getting ready for work.

personel 2:

Name: Rebecca Jones

Scenarios

  1. The CEO trying to look at the information of the weather in his office before taking his lunch break, so he would know what restaurant to eat at.
  2. The photographer wanting to take a picture outside, so she looks at the weather information to know how the weather is going to affect her picture.
  3. It is a winter season transitioning to the spring, and Rebecca would want to know when to go out to take a video shoot for her video content creation.
  4. At the autombile company, a car is to be tested outside and with the weather changing constantly, the CEO would have to create a date so the car can be testing. He checks the weather to find out what date to create.
