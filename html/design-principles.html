spotify.com

The principle of Alignment is one that when practiced, would give a website an artistic look. In Spotify's mobile site, the left-alignment was given to the footer. The left-alignment gave a clean design by leaving a form of white space as describe in the previous principle. This alignment is consistent through the footer section.
